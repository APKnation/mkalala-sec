Ordinary Level (Form 1)

  • Completed Standard 7 with NECTA PSLE results.

  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies).

Advanced Level (Form 5)

  • Form 4 CSEE certificates or Equivalent with required credits.
  • Selected combination must meet minimum credit requirements.
  • Character reference from the previous Head of School.
  • Valid Tanzanian ID or Passport for international students.
