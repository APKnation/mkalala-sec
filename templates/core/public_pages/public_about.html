Mkalala.

Mkalala Secondary School was born out of an idea to inject style and energy to education by changing how students learn all over the world. Our founder had a desire to modernize the educational landscape, leaving the stale and the conservative behind.

HISTORY OF THE BRAND

Mkalala first launched in the summer of 2005, in Stockholm and New York simultaneously. Our founder, John Mkalala, had a desire to challenge conventions and an idea to modernize the educational landscape, leaving the stale and the conservative behind.

Today, Mkalala has grown into a fashion and sports brand with a worldwide presence, offering innovative pieces within fashion, golf, ski, tennis, outdoor and more.

OUR PROMISE

We always leave a mark through the actions we take. By being bold and daring to do things differently, we push limits and redefine how things are done. We first introduced fashion into sports and then fused sports into fashion. It started with passion, but Mkalala is much more than that — it's a connection between cultures, ideas, activities, people and communities. Excellence is at the core of everything we do.
