Admission Requirements

What you need to join our school

Academic Requirements

Completed primary education with satisfactory grades. For Form I, students must have completed Standard VII with passing marks in national examinations.

Age Requirements

Students should be between 13-18 years old for Form I admission. Age requirements vary for different levels.

Required Documents

Birth certificate, previous school reports, national examination results, parent/guardian ID, and passport photos.

Medical Requirements

Complete medical examination form and proof of required vaccinations for school enrollment.
