Admission Requirements

Everything you need to know about our admission criteria.

Undergraduate

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum average grade C in PSLE results
  • Age: 13-15 years by January 2024
  • Good conduct and discipline record

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate (original + 2 copies)
  • PSLE results slip (original + 2 copies)
  • Primary school leaving certificate
  • Parent/guardian ID card (copies)
  • Recent passport photos (4 copies)
